Luka Crnič

I am a lecturer in linguistics in the Language, Logic and Cognition Center at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. I received my PhD in linguistics from MIT and, prior to that, studied linguistics, philosophy and mathematics at the University of Tübingen. My work, some of which you can download below, is primarily in semantics and its interfaces. For more details, you can look at my latest CV. You can also contact me via email.
