Luka Crnič

I am a lecturer in the Language, Logic and Cognition Center and the Department of Linguistics at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. I received my PhD in linguistics from MIT and, prior to that, studied linguistics, philosophy and mathematics at the University of Tübingen. My work, some of which you can download below, is primarily in semantics and its interfaces. For more information, you can look at my latest CV (updated 06/11/2016). You can contact me via email.
